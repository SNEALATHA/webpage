Services

Westernware

Westernware style is a blend of rugged and classic Western aesthetics, featuring cowboy boots, wide-brim hats, denim, leather jackets, and fringe details. It often includes embroidery, metal accents, and bold patterns inspired by the American frontier.

Straight-cut kurti

Kurti style blends traditional and modern fashion, offering a versatile and comfortable outfit for women. It comes in various designs, including A-line, straight-cut, Anarkali, and asymmetrical, often adorned with embroidery, prints, and embellishments

Croptops

Crop tops are trendy, short-length tops that add a chic and modern touch to any outfit. They come in various styles, including fitted, loose, off-shoulder, and tie-front, making them versatile for casual and party wear..

Duppata

Dupatta style varies from traditional to contemporary, enhancing ethnic outfits with elegance. It can be draped in different ways, such as over one shoulder, around the neck, or pleated for a regal look. Available in fabrics like chiffon, silk, and cotton, dupattas often feature embroidery, prints, or embellishments for added charm..

Jeans

Jeans style ranges from classic to trendy, offering a versatile and timeless fashion choice. Popular styles include skinny, straight-leg, bootcut, mom jeans, and distressed designs, catering to different looks and preferences.

Nightware

Nightwear combines comfort and style, offering soft and breathable fabrics for a restful sleep. Popular styles include pajama sets, nightgowns, satin slips, and cozy loungewear, designed for relaxation. With options ranging from simple cotton to luxurious silk, nightwear suits every preference and season.

Shirts

Explore stylish women's shirts—comfortable, versatile, and perfect for any occasion. Discover flattering fits, trendy designs, and premium fabrics today.

Short kurti

Trendy short kurtis for women—stylish, comfortable, and perfect for casual or festive wear. Pair with jeans or leggings effortlessly.

Tshirts
